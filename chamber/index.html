Ordinary General Assembly


The National Chamber of Commerce, Villahermosa held an Ordinary General Assembly, where Marco Antonio Gómez Figueroa was elected as the new president of the Board of Directors, for the period 2023-2024. During the Assembly, the new directors also protested, the vice-presidencies, commissions and delegations were ratified, as well as the administrative positions of the Chamber.
