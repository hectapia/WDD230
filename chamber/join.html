    Membership Levels

  • NP Membership

    As a non-profit organization, you will not pay any membership fees. This will also give you 1 disposition of our facilities for one event [8 hours] each 3 month [pre-scheduled] that you will host.

    Additionally, you will have access to the associates' catalog to receive special treatment for each service you request from them through VitaChamCo.

  • Bronze Membership

    Get a 10% discount for business-oriented training and 5% on advertising materials. This will also give you 1 disposition of our facilities for one event [8 hours] each month [pre-scheduled] that you will host, and media equipment will be provided.

    Enjoy these benefits of being a bronze member by an annual fee of $165+taxes.

  • Silver Membership

    Get a 20% discount for business-oriented training and 15% on advertising materials. This will also give you 2 free dispositions of our facilities for one event [8 hours] each month [pre-scheduled] that you will host, and media equipment will be provided.

    Enjoy these benefits of being a silver member by an annual fee of $350+taxes.

  • Gold Membership

    Get a 30% discount for business-oriented training and 20% on advertising materials. This will also give you 3 free dispositions of our facilities for one event [8 hours] each month [pre-scheduled] that you will host, and media equipment will be provided.

    Enjoy these benefits of being a gold member by an annual fee of $700+taxes
